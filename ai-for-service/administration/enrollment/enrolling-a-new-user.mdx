Use the **Invite** page to send join invitations to individual members of your organization. For inviting many users at once, use [Bulk Invite](./enrolling-many-users.mdx) instead.

![Inviting Users](../images/inviting-users-img1.png)

---

## Inviting Individual Users

1. In the **Enrollment** module, select the **Invite** page.
2. Enter the user's email address (for example, `john.doe@example.com`).
3. Select **Send Invitations**.

An invitation email is sent to the user. They must validate their email address before they appear as an active user in your account.

---

## Page Controls

| Control | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Email** | Enter the email address of the user to invite |
| **Clear All** | Removes all entered email addresses |
| **Send Invitations** | Sends invitation emails to all entered addresses |

<Note>You can invite up to 5 users at a time from the Invite page.</Note>
