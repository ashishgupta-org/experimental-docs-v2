The Summary Dashboard gives a snapshot of AI Agent conversations, user analytics, and performance over time.

## View the Summary Dashboard

1. Click **Analytics** on the left navigation pane. The Analytics panel displays the list of reports.

    ![Overview Dashboard](/ai-for-service/analytics/overview/images/overview-dashboard.png)

2. Click **Summary** under the **Overview** section of the Analytics panel. The Summary Dashboard appears on the right side of the page.
3. Select the appropriate filters and click **Apply**.

---

## Summary Dashboard Components

Once users start interacting with your assistant, the following sections appear on the Summary Dashboard.

- **Conversations Dashboard**: Displays key AI Agent metrics and insights into conversation usage and containment. Shows the conversation trend broken down by self-service, drop-off, and agent transfers. [Learn more](/ai-for-service/analytics/overview/conversations-dashboard).
- **Users Dashboard**: Displays trend-based analytics on new and returning users. [Learn more](/ai-for-service/analytics/overview/users-dashboard).
- **Performance Dashboard**: Provides insights on NLP and execution performance, including intent identification rate, goal completion rate, and service and script execution rates. [Learn more](/ai-for-service/analytics/automation/performance-dashboard).
