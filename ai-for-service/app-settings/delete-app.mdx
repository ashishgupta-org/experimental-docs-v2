The **Delete App** option allows you to delete your App.

To delete your app:

1. Click **App Settings** on the left-side menu.
2. Under **Configurations**, click **Delete App** at the bottom.

   ![Delete app](/ai-for-service/app-settings/images/delete-app-img1.png)

3. You can export the app prior to deletion. To do so, click **Export** in the Delete App dialog box.

   ![Delete app - export before deleting](/ai-for-service/app-settings/images/delete-app-img2.png)

4. To proceed with the deletion, click **Confirm**.

5. A verification code is sent to the app owner's registered email. Enter this code to confirm deletion.

   ![Delete app - confirm](/ai-for-service/app-settings/images/delete-app-img3.png)

6. After successful verification, the app is soft-deleted. A published app with active dialog tasks can be restored within 30 days from the admin console. If the app hasn't been published yet, the app will be deleted immediately.

<Note>Deletion is not allowed during an active subscription.</Note>
