---
title: Agent AI Upgrade from XO10 to XO11
---

This document provides step-by-step instructions to upgrade your Agent AI account to XO v11.

**Prerequisites**

- A valid Agent AI account.
- Bot Owner access is required to upgrade.

## What Changes

### Bot to App Transition

Agent AI bot and child bots become individual Apps in XO v11.

## Upgrade Journey from Agent AI XO10 to XO v11

### Upgrade Notification

1. Sign in to **Agent AI** > **Configurations**. A notification appears at the top of the screen.

   ![Agent AI Configurations](/ai-for-service/getting-started/images/agent-ai-configurations_1.png)

2. Click **Go to XO11** to start the upgrade.

    !!! note
        Only bot owners can upgrade Agent AI bots and child bots.

3. Sign in to [XO v11](https://platform.kore.ai/) using your Agent AI credentials.

   ![XO v11 Sign In](/ai-for-service/getting-started/images/xo11-signing-in_2.png)

4. All apps (Agent AI and automation bots) are displayed. Click the **Agent AI bot** to upgrade it to an App in XO v11.

   ![Upgrading App to XO v11](/ai-for-service/getting-started/images/upgrading-app-to-xo11_3.png)

5. A pop-up appears with **Why Upgrade?**, **Attention!**, and **Upgrade** options. Click **Continue**.

   ![Continue to Upgrade Pop-up](/ai-for-service/getting-started/images/continue-to-upgrade-popup_4.png)

6. Review the guidelines, download and back up your Agent AI bot, then click **Start Upgrade**.

   ![Start Upgrade](/ai-for-service/getting-started/images/start-upgrade_5.png)

    The backup contains only the bot definition.

7. A progress bar appears. Click **Finish** when complete.

   ![Finish Upgrade](/ai-for-service/getting-started/images/finish-upgrade_6.png)

8. A success message confirms the upgrade. The Agent AI bot is now an App in XO v11.

   ![Upgrade Success](/ai-for-service/getting-started/images/upgrade-success-message_7.png)

### Upgrade Failure

If an error occurs, the system stops the upgrade and lets you revert it.

1. An error notification appears in the upgrade window.

   ![Upgrade Error](/ai-for-service/getting-started/images/upgrade-error-message_8.png)

2. Click **Revert Upgrade**. You are redirected to the XO v11 home page.

   ![Revert Upgrade](/ai-for-service/getting-started/images/revert-upgrade_9.png)
