## Key Components

AI for Process is designed to provide a unified, flexible framework for implementing AI capabilities within an enterprise. It combines pre-built components, customization options, and integration capabilities to address a wide range of business needs while ensuring security and compliance.

![AI for Process Capabilities](/ai-for-process/images/ai-for-process-architecture.svg)

AI for Process enables organizations to automate complex business processes with AI.

### Workflows
Design and deploy AI-powered workflows with a visual builder. Combine AI nodes, integrations, and human-in-the-loop steps for end-to-end process automation.

- **Visual designer**: Drag-and-drop workflow creation.
- **Pre-built nodes**: AI, integration, logic, and utility nodes.
- **Branching**: Conditional paths and loops.
- **Error handling**: Built-in retry and fallback logic.

### Workflow Nodes
Use pre-built AI nodes for text generation, image processing, document intelligence, and more. Connect nodes to build sophisticated automation pipelines.

- **Text-to-Text**: Generation, summarization, extraction.
- **Text-to-Image**: Image generation from prompts.
- **Image-to-Text**: OCR, image description, analysis.
- **Audio-to-Text**: Transcription, speech recognition.
- **Doc Intelligence**: Document parsing and extraction.

### Inbox
Human-in-the-loop request management. Review, approve, and provide input for workflow decisions that require human judgment.

- **Approval workflows**: Human sign-off on AI decisions.
- **Review queues**: Centralized inbox for pending tasks.
- **Escalation**: Route complex cases to experts.
- **Feedback capture**: Improve AI with human corrections.

### Model Hub
Access open-source models, deploy fine-tuned models, and connect external model providers. Full control over AI model lifecycle.

- **Open-source models**: Access Hugging Face, custom models.
- **Fine-tuning**: Train models on your data.
- **External providers**: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google.
- **Model versioning**: Track and deploy model versions.

### Prompts
Design, test, and manage prompts with Prompt Studio. Version control, A/B testing, and collaborative prompt development.

## Use Cases

AI for Process automates complex business workflows across functional areas and industries. As an overlay automation layer, it integrates with your existing enterprise ecosystem without replacing current investments:

- ERP (SAP, Oracle)
- CRM (Salesforce, MS Dynamics)
- ITSM (ServiceNow, Zendesk)
- RPA (UiPath, Automation Anywhere, Power Automate)
- Data platforms and core systems

This integration approach helps you protect existing investments, expand automation coverage, reduce complexity and ownership cost, and unlock outcomes that no single system can achieve alone.

### Top Functional Use Cases

The platform follows a function-first approach, focusing on processes that deliver measurable outcomes:

| Function | Use Case | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Finance | Invoice processing | Faster cycle time, lower operational cost. |
| HR | Employee onboarding and access provisioning | Day-1 readiness, fewer manual handoffs. |
| IT | Zero-touch ticket resolution | Higher auto-closure of routine tickets. |
| Procurement | Supplier onboarding and management | Higher conversions, reduced manual follow-ups. |
| Customer Support | Complaint and escalation management | Significant reduction in non-compliance effort. |

### Additional Automation Scenarios

- Customer service workflows requiring intelligent routing and response.
- Document processing and data extraction tasks.
- Approval workflows with compliance requirements.
- Cross-system data synchronization and updates.
- Event-driven business process automation.
- Multi-step processes requiring coordination across departments or systems.
